## About This Account

[`@ewutc-admin`](https://github.com/ewutc-admin) is the institutional administrative account that owns and manages the [`@ewutchq`](https://github.com/ewutchq) GitHub Organization on behalf of **East West University Telecommunications Club**.

**This account is responsible for:**

- Ownership and configuration of the [`@ewutchq`](https://github.com/ewutchq) GitHub Organization
- Repository creation, archiving, and access management
- Team and member administration within the organization
- GitHub-level security, settings, and technical operations for the club
- Ensuring continuity of the club's digital infrastructure across executive terms

> **Note:** For EWUTC's club profile, events, announcements, and executive committee, visit the organization at [**github.com/ewutchq**](https://github.com/ewutchq).


<a id="maintained-by"></a>

## Maintained By

To ensure institutional continuity and long-term security, this account is maintained by the **EWUTC Executive Committee**. Access and day-to-day operations are overseen by the current Web Developer alongside authorized technical staff. Credentials and access are transferred securely at the start of each new executive term.

## The Organization

**East West University Telecommunications Club** was established on **14 April 2006** at East West University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. It is one of the university's most active student-run organizations, dedicated to advancing student knowledge in telecommunications, information technology, and professional career development.

EWUTC organizes workshops on IoT, Data Science, and Cyber Security; seminars on career development and digital innovation; and expert-led sessions by academic and industry professionals. <a href="https://github.com/ewutchq"><small>→ Visit the full organization profile at @ewutchq</small></a>
